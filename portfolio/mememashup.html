Meme Mashup

This is a meme mashup where I merged characters from the Marvel and DC universes in an unexpected way: two different meme mashups that have to do with the Marvel and DC world where different characters that don't belong to the original image are part of it, like the Joker in Guardians of the Galaxy and Taylor Swift replacing Wonder Woman in the DC world. The idea is to play with the identity of these characters and create funny and absurd situations that break with the conventions of their universes to which they really belong.
